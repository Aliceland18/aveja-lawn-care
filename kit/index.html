Where to post it

For a neighborhood lawn kid, Facebook + Nextdoor win. Do these with a parent.
  • Nextdoor — your Point of Woods neighborhood + nearby. #1 spot; parents hire local kids here all the time.
  • Facebook local groups — join "Manassas VA Community," "Manassas / Manassas Park," "Prince William County Community," and "Manassas Buy Sell Trade / Yard Sale."
  • Your HOA / neighborhood group — your literal neighbors = easy, close jobs.
  • Family shares — have Mom/Dad share your post once to their friends. Usually books your first few yards.
  • Football team parents — built-in customers who already root for you.
  • Flyers — library, coffee shops, gym, grocery entrance, HOA clubhouse, and door-to-door.

5 rules that win jobs

  • Reply FAST — answer texts/DMs within the hour. Fastest reply usually gets the job.
  • Show up on time — reliability is your whole brand. Prove it.
  • Take before/after pics of every job (ask first) — better than any graphic.
  • Be polite & professional in every message.
